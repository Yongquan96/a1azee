About Our Story

Started off as A1 Family Ayam Goreng & Western Food. Served many satisfied customers since 2000.
Renamed our brand to A1 AZEE Western Food in 2017

First started at Block 256 from 2000 till 2007.
Shifted to Block 293 Silver Spoon from 2007 till 2014.
Now located at A'Posh Bizhub from 2014 till now.

Our popular dish which includes the goreng pisang (fried banana), fried chicken wing, black pepper chicken chop, fish & chips, etc, has gather many love from all around the neighbourhood.
